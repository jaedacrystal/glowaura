Usage

Salicylic acid is used medically to treat acne, warts, psoriasis, dandruff, and calluses by exfoliating and softening the skin. Don't apply salicylic acid to raw or irritated skin, or to your eyes, lips, nose, mouth, or other sensitive areas.

Storage

Salicylic acid should be stored in a cool, dry, and well-ventilated area away from heat sources. It should be kept in a tightly closed container and disposed of in accordance with local, state, and federal environmental regulations.
